FAQ

A few common questions.

When do you step in?

I usually step in around 4–6 weeks before the wedding, once you have planned the main details and booked your suppliers.

Do you offer full wedding planning?

No — this service is focused on on-the-day wedding coordination, bringing together everything you have already planned.

How far do you travel?

Travel within 60 miles of Gloucestershire is included. Beyond that, mileage is charged at £0.45 per mile and accommodation may be required for extended travel.

Do you cover other events as well?

Yes — I also support parties, milestone celebrations, and other special events where calm coordination is needed.
